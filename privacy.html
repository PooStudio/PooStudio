Privacy
Policy

At POOSTUDIO, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and protection of your data.

01 — Information

Information We Collect

When you visit our website or use our services, we may collect certain information to provide and improve your experience.

  • Personal Information - Name, email address, and contact details you voluntarily provide to us.
  • Usage Data - IP addresses, browser type, pages visited, and timestamps of your interactions.
  • Device Information - Hardware model, operating system, and unique device identifiers.
  • Cookies - Small data files stored on your device to enhance functionality and remember your preferences.
02 - Usage

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely to deliver and improve our services, and never sold to third parties.

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our services and platform.
  • Improve, personalize, and expand your user experience.
  • Understand and analyze how you use our website.
  • Communicate with you regarding updates, support, or promotional content.
03 - Sharing

Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data only under the following circumstances.

  • Service Providers - Trusted third-party partners who assist in operating our platform, under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements - When required by law or to protect the rights and safety of POOSTUDIO and its users.
  • Business Transfers - In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.
04 - Security

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

05 - Rights

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data.

  • Access and receive a copy of your personal data.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Request deletion of your personal data under applicable law.
  • Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent.
06 - Contact

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please reach out to us. We are committed to resolving any concerns promptly and transparently.
